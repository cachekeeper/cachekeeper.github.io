CacheKeeper — Data Deletion

You can delete your CacheKeeper account and all associated data at any time using either of the methods below. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Option 1 — Delete from the app (instant)

Recommended

In-app deletion

Open CacheKeeper on your device, go to Settings → HomeSync → Delete Account. Your account and all cloud data will be deleted immediately. No email required.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

Email request

If you no longer have access to your device or cannot use the in-app option, send a deletion request to our support address. Include the email address associated with your account. We will complete deletion within 30 days.

Request deletion by email

What gets deleted

  • Your Firebase Authentication account and login credentials
  • All inventory items synced to the cloud via HomeSync
  • Your household membership and role
  • Any household you own (all members will lose access)

Not affected: Data stored locally on your device (inventory, photos, settings) is not deleted remotely — use Settings → Reset All Data in the app to clear your device.
